Studio 3

THE REALITY OF LIFE

Studio four takes us to the very reality of what our life is that in spite of everything that is happening around us, may it be joy. May it be sorrow. May it be anxious, you know, tension, or may it be just a cause of celebration. A photograph truly at the end captures that one particular feeling which cannot always be said. But it is something that is always felt. It is this very wistful and loving reality that we always want to wake up to. It is a wonderful jigsaw puzzle where we long to go on trips where we want to journey into the various hinterlands of the society, where we want to wake up every day and go to work, go to the markets. And it is a place where we want to live freely, without any doubt, without any anxiety without any tension. So whether it is Madiha Aijaz or Shilpa Gupta, Jyoti Bhatt or Sohrab Hura, these figurative photographs introduce us life in its very beautiful but also simple and banal manner.
